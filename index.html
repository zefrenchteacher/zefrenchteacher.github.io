Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you teach in Dublin, face-to-face?

    Since all the students find the classes more clear, organized and efficient online, all the classes have now been tailored for online only. Even the students who used to prefer face-to-face finally prefer Carole’s teaching online, finding Carole’s way of teaching online brilliant and very effective.

  • Does Ze French Teacher do individual tuitions?

    Tuitions are only offered for the French Oral Leaving Certificate exam preparation. The other grinds are tailored specifically for Groups (making sure they are also individually personalized). For the Weekly group grinds, Carole offers a monthly 45min FREE session, where the students can ask any question.

  • Is Ze French Teacher well aware of what is expected from the students doing French Junior Cycle exams and Leaving Certificate exams?

    Of course. Carole, on top of being a talented teacher who cares for everyone's students' success, knows the Syllabus, the Grading schemes and what the Examiners expect from the students perfectly. She was approached to teach in the most prestigious Secondary schools in Ireland.

  • How do the Personalized Group grinds work?

    Students find the group grinds very personalized. Indeed, each student sends to Carole her/his work two days ahead of the Group grind. Carole corrects and grades the student’s work and sends it back with some personalized notes ahead of the Group grind. During the grind, Carole clarifies everyone’s mistakes, making sure every student understands and improves during each grind.

  • What if the student has difficulties following the classes?

    Of course we all have different ways of learning and different speeds. That’s why Carole doesn’t take more than 12 students per group. She sends extra notes to students in need of extra help and welcome them to join the monthly 45 min FREE session to get tuition like help and support from Carole.

  • Why don’t you do bigger groups?

    In order to maintain the high standard of teaching and the best learning process for the students. The student’s success at the exams is Carole’s priority.
