Oral 1:1 (Leaving Certificate)

ORAL LEAVING CERTIFICATE PREPARATION

1:1 Tuition

PACKAGE 1h X 6 Sessions /PACKAGE 1h x 12 sessions
Goal: To get the student ready for her/his French Oral exam.

It runs from the 1st week of January to April.

  • We will write in French all the possible Sections that the students could be asked at the Oral exam(school,hobbies,holidays,...) and practice over and over.
  • They will receive those sections registered by Carole in mp3 format. The students find listening to this very useful.
  • We will correct pronounciation

We use Google Docs/Google Meet
