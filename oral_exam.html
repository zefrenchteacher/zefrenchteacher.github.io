Oral 1:1 (Leaving Cert)

ORAL LEAVING CERTIFICATE PREPARATION

1:1 Tuitions

PACKAGE 1h X 6 Sessions /PACKAGE 1h x 12 sessions

Goal: To get the student ready for her/his French Oral exam.

It runs from the 1st week of January to April. We use Google Meet (or Zoom) and Google Doc.

We write in French the Sections the students will be asked at the Oral:

  • Myself
  • My house/ area
  • My hobbies
  • My friends/ My Best Friend
  • A typical day/A Typical Weekend
  • My last holidays/weekend
  • My school
  • My next holiday/weekend
  • My future/career
  • Document
  • Society Topics

I will clarify what weâ€™ve written down with the student and will give the student efficient though easy-to-learn tools to get higher marks. I also teach her/him pronunciation with easy-to-learn techniques.

Finally, I record each section myself and send each section to the students, so the student can listen to it at home while reading the text. It helps the students to register the right pronunciation and it also trains their ears for the Aural Comprehension exam.
