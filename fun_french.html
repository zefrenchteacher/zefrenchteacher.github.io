Frequently Asked Questions

  • What Makes Ze French Teacher different from other French teachers?

    First of all, we are Native teachers, highly qualified and experienced. We’re also specialized in Junior Cert and Leaving Cert Exams (oral / aural comprehension / writing). Second, the students have access anytime to the classes, via Google Docs. Finally, on a more personal note, we really care about our student’s progress and genuinely want to help them improve. We understand they are under loads of pressure and making French fun seems to bring wonderful results. We bring a warm, fun, non-judgmental atmosphere for the students to want to learn. We simply care about our students and their grades and we do anything we can to support them.

  • How do you teach online ?

    Our students find our online teaching even better than face-to-face, clearer and better organized. We use Google Meet / Zoom as well as Google Doc to write instantly during the classes. We can easily highlight with colors, organize in a clearer way with tables… students really find it helpful. They find the visual plus of Online great and efficient.

  • How do we organize the classes?

    After classes, we give homework that is sent to you by email. For individual classes and Junior Cert / Leaving Cert Exam Papers Intensive, we then correct the homework together, making sure you are able then to do it on your own, understanding your own mistakes, being able then to correct yourself on your own.

  • Do you only teach online?

    Some of our teachers teach in Dublin 3. In face-to-face. We mostly teach online though.

  • Do you give a 10% discount to your old students?

    Of course we do.

  • Can I get refunded if I do not like the classes?
